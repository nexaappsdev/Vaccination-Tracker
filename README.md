# Vaccination Tracker 🌍

> Every vaccine, every country. Built for parents who refuse to give up their family's data.

A private, offline vaccine tracker for parents in **5 countries**. Track every
dose against the official national immunization schedule, get smart
reminders, and keep your child's records on your phone — never on a server.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🌍 Multi-Country Support
- Built-in schedules for **United States, Canada, Mexico, United Kingdom,
  and Australia** — pick your country once during onboarding
- Combination vaccines (5-in-1, 6-in-1, hexavalente, MMRV) automatically
  show what each shot covers
- Add a child in each country if your family spans borders — every child
  follows the schedule of their own country

### 📅 Official Government Schedules
- **🇺🇸 CDC** — Centers for Disease Control (2025–26 immunization schedule)
- **🇨🇦 NACI** — National Advisory Committee on Immunization (2024)
- **🇲🇽 PVU** — Programa de Vacunación Universal (2026, including COVID-19
  and Hepatitis A updates)
- **🇬🇧 NHS / JCVI** — Routine Childhood Immunisation Programme
- **🇦🇺 NIP / ATAGI** — National Immunisation Program

Sources are linked inside the app so you can verify any recommendation.

### 👶 Multiple Children, Multiple Countries
- Track unlimited children in one app
- Each child has their own country, region (state / province / nation),
  and timeline
- Switch between profiles with one tap

### 🔔 Smart Reminders
- One notification per visit — not one per vaccine — so you don't get
  spammed when 4 shots are due the same day
- Daily reminder for overdue doses
- Configurable lead time: 3, 7, or 15 days before due date
- Reminders fire reliably through Doze mode and after device reboot

### 📄 Share with Your Doctor
- Export any child's full vaccination record as a clean PDF
- Share via email, WhatsApp, Messages — anything that takes a file
- Perfect for school admission, daycare, travel, and pediatrician visits

### 🔁 Move Between Phones
- Generate a QR code with your child's full record
- Scan it on the new phone — profile and history transfer in one step
- No cloud, no account, no waiting

### 🧠 On-Device AI Assistant
- Ask "What's next for Emma?" or "Is anyone overdue?" in plain English
- Answers compose from your own records — no internet, no external AI call
- Pattern-matched locally; your questions never leave your phone

### 🔒 100% Private & Offline
- **No account.** No login. No "sign in with Google" — ever.
- **No internet.** The entire app works in airplane mode. Try it.
- **No analytics.** Zero SDKs. Zero pixels. Zero tracking.
- **No data shared with us.** We can't see your records because we never
  receive them.

### 🌐 7 Languages
- English, French, Spanish, Hindi, Punjabi, Mandarin, German
- Set explicitly from Settings — English is the default for v1.0

### 🎨 Designed for Daily Use
- Light & dark mode
- Tablet & foldable layouts (capped content width on large screens)
- Material You / Android 15 edge-to-edge
- Accessible — TalkBack-labeled, 48 dp touch targets, readable contrast

## 🌱 Why we built this

Tracking your kid's vaccines shouldn't require an account. It shouldn't
need an internet connection. And it definitely shouldn't sell your data
back to you in a "premium tier."

Vaccination Tracker was built around a single principle: **your family's
health records belong only on your phone.** Everything else — the
multi-country schedules, the AI assistant, the reminders — is built to
work entirely offline because that's the only way to actually guarantee
privacy.
