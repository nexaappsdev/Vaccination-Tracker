VaccineTracker USA

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2025  ·  Developer: Easio  ·  Contact: nexaapps.dev@gmail.com

🌱 100% offline · No data collection · No servers

VaccineTracker USA does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device. No accounts. No cloud. No tracking.

1. Who We Are

VaccineTracker USA is developed and published by Easio, an independent developer. This app is a personal record-keeping tool that helps parents in the United States track their children's CDC-recommended vaccinations entirely on-device.

2. Information We Store (Locally Only)

VaccineTracker USA does not collect any information. The following data is stored only on your device using an encrypted on-device SQLite database and never transmitted to any server:

  • Children's names and dates of birth
  • US state of residence (used to show state-specific school requirements)
  • Vaccination records including dates, provider names, lot numbers, and notes
  • Optional profile photos stored in private app storage
  • App preferences (theme, notification settings)

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to provide the app's features on your device:

  • Displaying your children's CDC vaccine schedules and tracking records
  • Calculating overdue and upcoming doses based on CDC 2025 guidelines
  • Generating PDF reports for personal use or sharing with healthcare providers
  • Sending local push notifications for upcoming vaccine doses
  • Showing state-specific school immunization requirements

No data is used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose beyond what you see in the app.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. There are no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or external services integrated into VaccineTracker USA. Your data is yours alone.

5. Internet Access

VaccineTracker USA is fully functional without an internet connection and does not communicate with any server. The only network activity occurs when you choose to tap external links (such as links to the CDC website), which opens your browser outside the app.

6. Camera & Photo Library

VaccineTracker USA may request access to your camera or photo library for two purposes:

  • Profile photos: Optionally attach a photo to a child's profile. Stored locally only.
  • Record scanning (Premium): Use your camera to photograph paper vaccination records for easier manual entry. Images are processed on-device and never uploaded.

Camera access is always optional. You may deny camera permission and still use all core features of the app.

7. Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, VaccineTracker USA uses your device's local notification system to remind you of upcoming vaccine doses. Notification scheduling is handled entirely on-device. No notification content or schedule is sent to any server.

8. In-App Purchases

VaccineTracker USA offers an optional one-time premium upgrade through Google Play Billing. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not process, store, or have access to any payment information. For purchase support, contact Google Play directly.

9. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

VaccineTracker USA complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not collect any personal information from children. The app is operated by parents on behalf of their children — all data is entered by the parent and stored locally on the parent's device only.

Because VaccineTracker USA operates entirely offline with no server communication, no child data ever leaves the parent's device. We have no ability to access, view, or process any information about any child.

10. Data Security

All data is stored in a private on-device SQLite database accessible only to the VaccineTracker USA app. No data is stored in shared or publicly accessible locations on your device.

11. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Delete individual vaccination records from any child's profile
  • Remove a child's profile and all associated records
  • Wipe all data at once via Settings → Delete All Data
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data from your device

Because data is stored only on your device, there is no server-side deletion required.

12. Third-Party Libraries

VaccineTracker USA integrates the following on-device libraries. None transmit personal information externally:

  • Google Play Billing — handles in-app purchase transactions via Google Play
  • Expo Notifications — schedules local push notifications on-device
  • Expo Camera / Image Picker — accesses device camera and photo library on permission
  • Expo SQLite — stores all app data in a local on-device database

13. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
